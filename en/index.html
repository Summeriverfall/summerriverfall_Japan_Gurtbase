About

Your bowl, your way —
right in the heart of Kyoto

GurtBase is a self-serve frozen yogurt and açaí shop in Kyoto's Sanjo district. Choose from 4 cup sizes and over 30 toppings to build your perfect bowl — fresh fruit, granola, nuts, sauces, and more. Pay by weight at just ¥4/g, so you control exactly what goes in.
