About

Your bowl, your way —
right in the heart of Kyoto

GurtBase is a frozen yogurt and açaí shop in Kyoto's Sanjo district. Choose from fixed-size cups (100g, 150g, 200g) and over 30 toppings paired into signature sets — fresh fruit, granola, nuts, sauces, and more. Plain Greek yogurt starts at just ¥700, so you can enjoy your bowl affordably.
